About Us

Payment Nepal is a financial technology (E-commerce) startup company that is endorsed by Nepal Rastra Bank (The Central Bank). Our first mission is to create a cross border payment infrastructure through the internet. The infrastructure is used by Small Medium Enterprises (SME) of Nepal having a potential to sell their services or products to the audience (customer) across border. SME uses our software to accept payments and manage their businesses online.
Our sole ambition is to create a bridge between people and technology in order to increase the foreign currency earning of Nepal. We endeavor to make the internet a soul of our economy. Our ambition could be a metaphor of positive upheaval in the way we do the cross border business and bring into fore entrepreneurs from every nook and corner of the country.
